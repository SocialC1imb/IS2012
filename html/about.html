Singing and Music


I have been singing for as long as I can remember, and although I don't take lessons or perform (often) anymore, I don't plan on stopping! I sing mainly, but I also play a handful of instruments, such as guitar, bass, piano, and drums. I was a part of the School of Rock - Plano performance group for quite a few years and was even in my own band with a couple of friends (and I aam thinking about starting another). I actually have one song released on streaming platforms too, under the name of "Sky Toups"!
